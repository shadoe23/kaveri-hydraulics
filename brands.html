LEAKOLOCK

Sealing Systems for Heavy Equipment

Premier Products

LEAKOLOCK Brand seal kits are the preferred after-market alternative to the original equipment manufacturer. As a premier provider of sealing systems for heavy equipment, LEAKOLOCK Brand seal kits support excavator parts distribution companies, and heavy equipment operators and resellers around the country.

LEAKOLOCK Brand seal kits are designed and manufactured for the critical and demanding hydraulic cylinder, transmission, pumps and motor applications found in heavy equipment.

Engineered Solutions

With decades of experience, LEAKOLOCK knows exactly how to solve sealing problems. Standard and custom designs are quickly engineered using state-of-the-art measuring and inspection equipment partnered with computer aided design and drafting tools.

These design tools are combined with CNC-operated manufacturing equipment, giving LEAKOLOCK the capacity for high-volume production along with the flexibility to produce seals for prototype and low-volume applications.

Industry Leading Inventory

LEAKOLOCK's facility in Bangalore was specifically designed to support the manufacturing and assembly of a wide range of cylinder seal kits and O-ring kits. With a 14,000 ft² storage facility, several thousand products are actively stocked and immediately available.

With automated kit assembly and packaging technology, thousands more are available within only a few days. Whether from our catalogue of existing products or a custom tailored solution, LEAKOLOCK can provide a sealing system to fit your needs.

Commitment

While many things have changed, LEAKOLOCK remains committed to the same long-standing goals — providing high quality products with unmatched customer service. LEAKOLOCK Brand products are now sold across the country.

After almost 25 years of operations, a focus on providing high-quality products has led many businesses around the world to demand the LEAKOLOCK Brand seal kits.

SAKURA

Filtration & Fluid Management

Sakura Filters

When it comes to filtration — filter out the rest and stay with the best.

Sakura Filters offer the widest range of quality filtration products available globally, supported by excellent service. Whether for hydraulic systems, engine oil or fuel, Sakura has a filter solution engineered to the precise standard required.

Sakura Oil Filters

Sakura oil filters are made to the exact standard to reduce frictional wear by eliminating particles released from friction parts and combustion. This enhances the efficiency and life expectancy of the engine, protecting critical components from premature wear.

By maintaining clean oil throughout the system, Sakura filters help reduce maintenance costs and maximise uptime for your excavator fleet.
